Combination March

Lucas Werkmeister
, 2017-07-29.

Scott Joplin’s Combination March, one of his first piano publications. It may be simple, but I quite enjoy it.

(The recording isn’t perfect, but it seems to be the best I can manage in a single take. I’ve been meaning to record this for some time, but this is the first take with few enough mistakes that I’m happy publishing it.)
