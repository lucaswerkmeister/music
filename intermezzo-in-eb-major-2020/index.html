Intermezzo in E♭ major

Lucas Werkmeister
, 2020-12-30.

For the Wikipaka assembly at rC3, I made a recording of the Intermezzo Nr. 1, the first of Johannes Brahms’ Drei Intermezzi. It’s not perfect, but good enough that I thought I’d upload it here.

Alternate version

I made two recordings. The one above is the first one, which I think has slightly more minor blunders but no larger problems; the following one worked better overall, but I made a larger mistake at the beginning of the “third” part, which is why I’m relegating it to the “alternate” version. (I sent that version to Wikipaka, though, since I thought it overall sounded better and the mistake wasn’t so important in that context.)
