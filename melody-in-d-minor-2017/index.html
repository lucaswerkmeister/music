Melody in d minor

Lucas Werkmeister
, 2017-07-29.

You can find the backstory of this piece in my post on the Melody in f minor – this is the melody I had come up with and then forgotten. It turned out to be more similar than I thought.

In fact, I remembered the melody about half an hour after putting up that recording, and excitedly wrote it down before going to bed. However, now that I’d written it down there was no longer any need to record the melody immediately, which is why it’s taken me almost seven weeks to upload this.
