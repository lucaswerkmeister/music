Tango (Improvisation)

Lucas Werkmeister
, 2017-12-14.

(TL;DR: the recording gets more interesting after 0:35.)

My friend Merle lent me some of her piano training books, and in The European Piano Method (Volume 2) by Fritz Emonts I found this Tango, where you’re supposed to complete the accompaniment yourself. I took a bit more liberty ☺

The recording starts out with me just playing what’s in the actual sheet music, which isn’t very much. The second time round I play what I imagine they expect you to play, more or less. After that, I went a bit off track, and improvised – mainly on the “tango” topic, but you’ll hear some deviations, and you might even recognize some entirely different compositions in there.
